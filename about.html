About the Book

*The Rise and Fall of Dona Belita* tells the powerful story of Bertha Riziki Mavoa, affectionately known as Dona Belita. Through harrowing challenges such as eviction, fire, and illness, Dona Belita’s life exemplifies the unbreakable strength of the human spirit. Her son, Micka Stephano Mavoa, shares her story with heartfelt dedication, completing the mission she entrusted to him.

Why This Story Matters

This memoir is more than a story of personal resilience; it is a testament to love, sacrifice, and the strength found in family. Dona Belita’s journey serves as an inspiration for all who face hardship, reminding us that even in our darkest hours, there is hope and a path forward.

Our Mission

Beyond telling Dona Belita’s story, we are committed to supporting women who face similar struggles. A portion of the proceeds from this book goes toward empowering women through education, workshops, and seminars. Our programs create safe spaces for women to share their experiences, find healing, and build a supportive community.

Get Involved

By purchasing this book or making a donation, you directly support initiatives that help women overcome adversity. We also invite volunteers worldwide to join our cause and make a lasting difference in the lives of women who need it most.
