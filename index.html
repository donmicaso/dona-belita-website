About the Book

*The Rise and Fall of Dona Belita* tells the journey of Bertha Riziki Mavoa, a woman who overcame incredible odds. Join her as she faces eviction, fire, and slow poisoning, emerging as a beacon of resilience.

Book Highlights

“When I looked at everything I had built and lost, I reminded myself: strength is born out of survival.”

“Through every challenge, I found resilience I didn’t know I had.”

About Dona Belita

Dona Belita’s life is a story of survival and strength. From humble beginnings to fierce battles for justice, her journey embodies resilience. This book not only tells her story but also supports women who face similar struggles.

Our Mission

With every purchase and donation, you help educate and empower women who have experienced adversity. Our programs provide seminars, workshops, and safe spaces where women can share their stories, heal, and support one another. Volunteers worldwide are invited to join us in making a difference.
