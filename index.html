About the Book

*The Rise and Fall of Dona Belita* tells the journey of Bertha Riziki Mavoa, a woman who overcame incredible odds. Join her as she faces eviction, fire, and slow poisoning, emerging as a beacon of resilience.

Book Highlights

“When I looked at everything I had built and lost, I reminded myself: strength is born out of survival.”

“Through every challenge, I found resilience I didn’t know I had.”
