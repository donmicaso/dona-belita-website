About the Book

The Rise and Fall of Dona Belita tells the journey of Bertha Riziki Mavoa, a woman who overcame incredible odds. Join her as she faces eviction, fire, and slow poisoning, emerging as a beacon of resilience.

Book Highlights

“When I looked at everything I had built and lost, I reminded myself: strength is born out of survival.”

“Through every challenge, I found resilience I didn’t know I had.”

About the Book

This memoir is more than a personal journey; it’s a testament to resilience, love, and strength. Dona Belita’s life story serves as an inspiration, reminding readers of the hope and courage that exists within all of us, even in our darkest hours.

Our Mission

With every purchase and donation, we support educational seminars and healing workshops for women facing similar struggles, offering them safe spaces to share, heal, and find community support.

About the Author

Micka Stephano Mavoa, the son of Bertha Riziki Mavoa, was entrusted with completing his mother's story. He witnessed her journey firsthand and continues to share her legacy, hoping to inspire others through her strength and resilience.
